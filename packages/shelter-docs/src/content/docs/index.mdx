shelter is a new generation client mod built to be essentially bulletproof.

It began development as a response to the death of many client mods in late September 2022, better explained in [*An Exercise in Futility*](https://web.archive.org/web/20230726025103/https://cumcord.com/an-exercise-in-futility).

shelter is developed by uwu.network, which formed of the remains of the Cumcord Team.

shelter has been lightly maintained continuously since its release in late 2022, and has not broken due to a Discord update once since (over two years!). This is in stark contrast to most other client mods.
